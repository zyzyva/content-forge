## 2. Viral Hooks Prompt

Act as a viral content strategist who has created 1,000,000 different viral hooks. Generate scroll-stopping hooks based on the context below. Make them short, punchy, psychologically compelling, and optimized for the platforms I am posting on. For each hook, also provide 1–2 sentences of context or inspiration for how it could be expanded into a video. If I am calling out an ideal customer profile, I want to be specific about who that is.

Explain why this set of hooks is highly likely to work and go viral given the context.

**Context:**

- Industry/Niche
- Target Audience: Age, profession, interests, pain points, aspirations, etc.
- Brand Tone/Voice: Professional, motivational, entertaining, edgy, humorous, inspirational, etc.
- Content Type/Goal: Educational, storytelling, testimonial, controversial, behind-the-scenes, thought leadership, etc.
- Platform Focus (optional): TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, LinkedIn, or multiple platforms
- Number of hooks

## 3. Viral Script Prompt

Act as a content expert who has created 100,000,000 viral, high-converting videos in my industry / niche. Use only the information in the context section below to generate all outputs.

**Context:**

- Industry / Niche: e.g., real estate, finance, mindset, business consulting
- Video Length: e.g., 30–60 seconds, 45-second Instagram Reel
- Hook / Idea: [use the short form content machine if you need help with hooks / ideation]
- Audience: [target audience]
- Platform: [Instagram Reels / TikTok / YouTube Shorts / LinkedIn]
- Tone: [motivational / contrarian / story-driven / educational / conversational]
- Style / Inspiration: [Alex Hormozi / Mel Robbins / any creator]
- Other Context / Details: [optional — product info, personal story, trends, pain points, key messaging]

Using only the information in the context section:

1. Create a full video script matching the specified length and industry.
2. Make the opening hook grab attention in the first 3 seconds.
3. Write a story-driven or educational narrative that flows naturally from the hook/idea.
4. Include captions or overlay text to boost engagement and retention.
5. Suggest optional B-roll or visual ideas aligned with the context.
6. End with a strong call-to-action appropriate for the audience and platform.

Finally, explain why this video will work, referencing engagement, relatability, virality, and audience resonance.

## 4. Prompt to Find Example Pages

> [Write a paragraph about the type of social media page or content focus you want analyzed. For example, "Founder-led Instagram for a local chiropractic office" or "Local bakery page highlighting employees and products"]

Act as a top industry expert and social media content analyst who has identified 1,000,000 viral pieces of content in the niche and platform listed above where the focus and tones match the type specified above. Analyze accounts that have created content that performed exceptionally well, and break down why the content worked. Examine psychology, storytelling, hooks, structure, delivery, angles, emotions, and engagement patterns.

For each account, provide:

- Direct link to their page
- Direct link to their standout post or posts
- Deep explanation of WHY the post worked, including:
  - Hook effectiveness
  - Credibility effectiveness
  - Emotion triggered
  - Format structure (cuts, pacing, captions, visuals, audio)
  - Value delivered (education, entertainment, inspiration, trust-building, etc.)
  - Angle or perspective that made it unique
  - Psychological drivers behind engagement

After listing the accounts, provide a top-level analysis summarizing:

- The recurring elements that made these pieces perform
- The storytelling patterns that consistently generate engagement
- What creators or pages should replicate to achieve similar results
- What to avoid based on what you observed

Keep the focus entirely on why the content performed, not trends, data swings, or platform shifts.

## 5. Content Calendar Prompt

I need you to act as my head of marketing who has created 1,000,000 pieces of viral content for other creators like myself and create a content rollout strategy for my personal brand and company. Use the context I provide below to make it highly personalized, actionable, and designed to convert followers into raving fans who purchase multiple times and refer others.

**Context:**

- Number of Days / Posts: [e.g., 100-day content plan]
- Brand / Company Info & Links: [Insert links, website, social profiles, company details]
- Target Audience: [Age, interests, pain points, platforms they use, behavior]
- Brand Voice / Personality: [e.g., professional, humorous, inspirational, edgy, casual]
- Key Products / Services / Offers: [What you want to promote]
- Content Goals: [e.g., audience growth, engagement, conversion, brand awareness]
- Content Styles / Formats: [e.g., talking head, POV, skits, carousels, reels, threads]
- Content to Avoid: [Topics, styles, formats, or messages to avoid]
- Hooks I Want to Include: [Any hook ideas you already have]
- Content Pillars / Main Themes: [3–5 core topics you want to cover repeatedly]
- Preferred CTAs: [e.g., "Book a call," "Download free guide," "Comment your thoughts"]
- Posting Frequency / Constraints: [How many posts per week, max duration, etc.]

**Your Task:**

Using the context above, create a content plan where each post includes:

- Hook — an attention-grabbing opening
- Topic — what the content is about
- Format — based on user preferences (talking head, POV, carousel, skit, etc.)
- What to Record / Say / Show — instructions for the creator to follow
- CTA — a clear call-to-action aligned with the user's goals
- Rationale — explain why this content works for the target audience, moves them along the journey from knowing nothing to becoming a raving fan/customer, and how it aligns with goals

Research publicly available information about the user and their company based on the context provided. Ensure the content is highly personalized, engaging, and strategically effective.
